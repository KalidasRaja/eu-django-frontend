6Storage is a proud member of Self-Storage associations across the globe.

Our Self-Storage Associations

Federation of European Self Storage Associations (FEDESSA)

Federation of European Self Storage Associations, We represent over 2000+ self-storage facilities which are over 70 % of self-storage facilities in Europe, including all the major operators.
Self Storage Association Asia (SSAA)

The Self Storage Association Asia (SSAA) is to help grow and protect our members businesses as well as the Asian storage sector through research, education, government advocacy, key introductions, and high level networking opportunities.
Self Storage Association (SSA)

The Self Storage Association is a non-profit organization that represents the industry's 50,000+ facilities before the United States Congress and federal departments and agencies.
Texas Self Storage Association (TXSSA)

TSSA is the country's largest state self-storage association, offering a wide range of benefits to self-storage owners and operators.
Kansas Self Storage Owners Association (KSSOA)

KSSOA is established to develop the Kansas self-storage industry, promote professional standards and quality, and provide a united voice on problems impacting the industry in the state and its communities.
Missouri Self Storage Owners Association (MSSOA)

MSSOA is created to assist and safeguard self-storage facility owners and operators in our state. And it is filled with education, networking, and fun.
